San Romero de America

The man who chose to put the most vulnerable people first and spoke for them...
Official image of Saint Oscar Arnulfo Romero: Bishop and Martyr

    Timeline of bishop Orscar Romero prior his martyrdom:

  • 1917-Oscar Arnulfo Romero was born into a family of ten on 15 August in Ciudad Baririos, El Salvador
  • 1930-When he was 14 years old, Oscar wanted to be a priest so he went to study at a junior seminary
  • 1937-Oscar went to Rome to study and stayed there during World War II. His father and brother died while he was in Rome
  • 1942-Romero was ordained a priest and returned to El Salvador. He became famous for his sermons
  • 1970-Romero was made a bishop in 21 June
  • 1974-Violence increased as the government and army began killing poor people who stood up for their rights
  • 1977-Romero became Archbishop of San Salvador. Rich people thought he would stop priests from helping the poor to stand up...
  • 1977-80-Every sunday his sermon was broadcast by radio. The whole country listened. His life was often threatened
  • 1980-In his sermon on 23 March: "In the name of God, and in the name of this suffering people whose cries rise to heaven ...
  • 2009-On 15 March, the new president of El Salvador announced that his goverment would be inspired by Romero: "option for the poor"
  • 2015-Oscar was beatified in El Salvador on 23 May: "a friend in faith, who invoke him in as protector and intercessor...
  • 2017-Marked the centenary of the birth of Blessed Oscar Romero
  • 2018-Pope Francis approved a miracle which has been attributed to Oscar Romero.
